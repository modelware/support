# 🛠️ Modelware Support

Welcome to the official Modelware support repository. This is the place to report issues, ask questions, and track the status of your requests across all Modelware products.

---

## 📋 How to Submit a Support Ticket

1. Navigate to the [**Issues**](../../issues) tab above.
2. Click **New Issue**.
3. Select the appropriate issue template for your request type.
4. Fill in all required fields — the more detail you provide, the faster we can help.
5. Submit and **note your issue number** for future reference.

> ⚠️ **Please search existing issues before submitting** to avoid duplicates. Your question may already be answered!

---

## 🗂️ Issue Templates

| Template | Use When |
|---|---|
| 🐛 **Bug Report** | Something isn't working as expected |
| 💡 **Feature Request** | You'd like to suggest an improvement or new capability |
| ❓ **General Question** | You need help understanding how something works |
| 🔐 **Security Vulnerability** | You've discovered a potential security issue *(see below)* |

---

## 🔐 Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

**Please do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.**

Report security issues directly to our team at **support@modelware.io**. We take all reports seriously and will respond within 48 hours.

---

## ⏱️ Response Times

| Priority | Description | Target Response |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 Critical | Service down / data loss | Within 1 business day |
| 🟠 High | Major feature broken | Within 2 business day |
| 🟡 Medium | Degraded functionality | Within 5 business days |
| 🟢 Low | Questions / minor issues | Within 10 business days |

*Response times apply during business hours: Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm (ET).*
